# Class 4 (4th Standard) Content & Video Research

## Executive Summary

This document provides comprehensive research on video and content requirements for Class 4 (Grade 4) students for the StreamShaala educational app. It covers the complete NCERT/CBSE curriculum, gap analysis with existing content, and recommendations for content expansion.

**Currently in App (5 chapters - Custom):**
1. Large Numbers (place value, comparing)

3. Multiplication and Division
4. Fractions
5. Geometry

**Missing NCERT Chapters (Need to Add):**
1. Building with Bricks (3D shapes)
2. Long and Short (measurement)
3. A Trip to Bhopal (distances)
4. Tick-Tick-Tick (time)
5. The Way The World Looks (maps)
6. Jugs and Mugs (capacity)
7. Carts and Wheels (circles)
8. How Heavy? How Light? (weight)
9. Smart Charts (data handling)

**Note:** Current chapters partially overlap with NCERT but use different structure. Recommend aligning with NCERT chapter names for better recognition.

## Appendix A: Learning Objectives by Subject

### Mathematics Learning Outcomes (NCF 2005)
- Understand numbers up to 1,00,000
- Perform four basic operations
- Understand fractions and decimals basics
- Measure length, weight, capacity, time
- Recognize 2D and 3D shapes
- Read and create simple graphs
- Solve word problems

### EVS Learning Outcomes
- Understand family and community relationships
- Learn about plants, animals, and ecosystems
- Understand water cycle and conservation
- Learn about food, nutrition, and health
- Understand cultural diversity
- Develop observation and inquiry skills

### English Learning Outcomes
- Read and comprehend grade-appropriate texts
- Write simple paragraphs and letters
- Use correct grammar in sentences
- Develop vocabulary (500+ new words)
- Recite poems with expression
- Participate in conversations

### Hindi Learning Outcomes
- Read and write Hindi fluently
- Understand Hindi grammar basics
- Comprehend stories and poems
- Write short compositions
- Develop Hindi vocabulary
