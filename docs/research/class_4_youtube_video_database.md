## Part 4: Video Integration Strategy

### 4.1 Primary vs Secondary Videos

**Primary Videos (Use First):**
- Magnet Brains for all NCERT chapters
- Complete chapter coverage
- Hindi medium (target audience)
- Exam-oriented

**Secondary Videos (Supplementary):**
- International channels for concepts
- English medium options
- Fun/engaging content
- Visual/animated explanations

### 4.2 Video Selection Criteria

| Criteria | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Duration | 5-15 minutes ideal |
| Language | Hindi (primary), English (secondary) |
| Quality | 720p minimum, 1080p preferred |
| Engagement | Animation/visual aids |
| Accuracy | NCERT aligned |
| Ads | Minimal/No mid-roll ads |

### 4.3 Recommended Video Count Per Topic

| Difficulty | Videos | Primary | Secondary |
|------------|--------|---------|-----------|
| Basic | 4-5 | 2-3 | 1-2 |
| Intermediate | 5-6 | 3-4 | 1-2 |
| Advanced | 3-4 | 2 | 1-2 |
