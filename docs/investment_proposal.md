## Why This Structure Works

### For Investor:
1. **Downside Protection**: ₹1 Cr loan is repayable regardless of company performance
2. **Upside Participation**: 10% equity captures growth potential
3. **Hybrid Risk**: Lower risk than pure equity, better return than pure debt
4. **Aligned Compensation**: Founder salary increases only when business grows

### For Founder:
1. **Equity Preservation**: Retain 90% ownership
2. **Sustainable Salary**: ₹30L starting, grows to ₹54L+ with milestones
3. **Aligned Incentives**: Salary tied to performance, both parties benefit from growth
4. **Family Security**: Stable income while building the company

## About StreamShaala

StreamShaala is an AI-powered educational platform designed for K-12 students in India. The platform offers:

- **Adaptive Learning**: AI-driven gap analysis and personalized learning paths
- **Comprehensive Content**: Video lessons, quizzes, flashcards, and study materials
- **Gamification**: XP system, badges, streaks, and leaderboards to boost engagement
- **Offline-First**: Works without internet, syncs when connected
- **Multi-Platform**: Available on all major platforms from a single codebase

### Competitive Advantages

1. **Near-Zero Server Costs**: Offline-first architecture eliminates expensive cloud infrastructure
2. **Multi-Platform**: Single codebase serves 6 platforms (vs competitors' separate apps)
3. **AI Pedagogy**: Spaced repetition and gap analysis for effective learning
4. **Privacy-Focused**: User data stored locally, optional cloud sync via Google Drive
