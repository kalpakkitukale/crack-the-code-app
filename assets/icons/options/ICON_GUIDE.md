## 💡 Tips for Choosing:

- **Visual Distinction:** Make sure each flavor looks different from the others
- **Target Audience:** Consider which design fits the age group best
- **App Purpose:** Remember this is an educational video streaming app
- **Brand Consistency:** Try to maintain some visual connection between flavors
- **Professional Look:** Icons should look polished on app stores

Take your time reviewing all options! 🎨
